The Trusted Community Contracting Company in Lane County, Oregon

Multi Contracting, LLC has been the roofing and exterior contracting choice for Eugene homeowners for over 20 years. Based at 950 Tyinn St in Eugene, our team serves Lane County with honest work and certified quality you can count on.

From roof repair to full replacements, siding, fencing, painting, and custom concrete curbing, we handle your entire exterior under one roof — so you can trust that every part of the job is done right, on time, and within budget.

Our bilingual team serves English and Spanish-speaking homeowners throughout Eugene, Springfield, and Santa Clara, OR. Se Habla Español. CCB#238954 — Licensed, Bonded & Insured.
