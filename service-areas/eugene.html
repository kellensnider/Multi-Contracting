Eugene's climate is unique: heavy fall and winter rainfall, periodic ice storms, and damp springs create challenging conditions for roofing and exterior building materials. Moss growth on roofs, wood rot in aging siding, and ice damming in cold snaps are all common issues we address regularly for Eugene homeowners. Our crews understand this climate and choose materials and techniques specifically suited to performing well here.
