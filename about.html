About Multi Contracting, LLC

Over 20 years of trusted roofing and exterior contracting in Eugene, OR and Lane County.

Our Story

Built on Trust, Backed by Experience

Multi Contracting, LLC was founded right here in Eugene, Oregon with a simple mission: deliver honest, high-quality exterior contracting services at a fair price. Over two decades later, that mission still drives everything we do — from a quick roof patch to a complete commercial roofing installation.

Our team is composed of experienced, certified professionals who take genuine pride in their craft. We're not a faceless franchise or out-of-town contractor — we're your neighbors. When we work on your home or business, we treat it the way we'd treat our own.

As a bilingual company (English & Spanish), we're able to serve the full diversity of Lane County's community. No matter where you're from or what language you speak, you'll receive the same attentive, professional service we've built our reputation on.
