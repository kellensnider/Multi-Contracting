    Define Your Landscape with Custom Concrete Curbing

    Concrete landscape curbing is one of the smartest investments you can make in your property's exterior. It creates clean, defined borders between lawn areas, garden beds, driveways, and walkways — eliminating the need for constant edging while simultaneously boosting curb appeal and property value. Unlike plastic edging that shifts and fades, concrete curbing is permanent, maintenance-free, and looks great year after year.

    Multi Contracting, LLC installs custom curbing designs for homeowners and commercial property managers throughout Eugene, Springfield, and Santa Clara, OR. Our curbing is poured on-site in continuous mold extrusion, creating smooth, seamless edges that follow the exact curves and lines of your landscape. We offer a variety of profiles, colors, and decorative finishes to match your property's style.

    Curbing can be installed as a standalone project or bundled with exterior painting, siding, or other Multi Contracting services for a comprehensive exterior refresh. Call (541) 600-9570 for a free curbing estimate in Lane County, OR.

    Our Curbing Installation Process

    1
    Design Consultation

    We walk your property with you, discuss the areas to be edged, review profile and color options, and provide a free written estimate.

    2
    Layout & Preparation

    We mark the curbing path according to your design plan and prepare the ground along the installation line.

    3
    On-Site Concrete Extrusion

    We use a curbing machine to extrude continuous concrete curbing directly on your property — no seams, no shifting sections.

    4
    Finishing & Sealing

    We apply expansion cuts to prevent cracking, stamp or texture as requested, and seal the curbing to protect its color and extend its lifespan.
