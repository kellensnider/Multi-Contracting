Roof Replacement in Eugene, OR

When repairs are no longer enough, Multi Contracting, LLC delivers complete roof replacements — all roofing types, superior materials, and certified installation backed by manufacturer warranties.

Roof Replacement Eugene, OR

A New Roof, Built to Last Decades

A quality roof replacement is one of the most valuable investments you can make in your Eugene, Oregon home or commercial property. The Pacific Northwest climate — with its heavy annual rainfall, occasional ice storms, and damp winters — demands a roof that performs reliably year after year. At Multi Contracting, LLC, we specialize in matching the right roofing system to your property's unique needs, budget, and style.

As a GAF Master Elite certified and Owens Corning Preferred contractor, we install roofing systems that qualify for the strongest available manufacturer warranties in the industry. We offer asphalt shingles, standing seam metal, concrete and clay tile, and flat roofing membrane systems — giving Eugene and Lane County homeowners a full range of options at every price point.

Every replacement project begins with a thorough inspection of your existing roof deck, ventilation, and structural system. We remove and properly dispose of the old roofing, inspect and repair the deck if needed, and install the new system according to manufacturer specifications. Roofing Materials We Install

🏠
Asphalt Shingles

The most popular roofing material in the US — cost-effective, durable, and available in dozens of styles and colors. We install architectural and designer shingles from GAF and Owens Corning.

🔩
Metal Roofing

Standing seam metal roofs last 40–70 years, shed water and snow effectively, and offer excellent energy efficiency. Ideal for Eugene's wet climate.

🏗️
Tile Roofing

Concrete and clay tile adds lasting beauty and exceptional durability. Tile roofs are fire-resistant and can last 50+ years with proper maintenance.

🌧️
Flat / Low-Slope Roofing

TPO and EPDM rubber membrane systems designed specifically for flat or low-pitched roofs on residential additions and commercial buildings.

Our Replacement Process

1
Free Inspection & Estimate

We inspect your roof, identify structural issues, and provide a detailed written estimate — no obligation required.

2
Material Selection

We help you choose the best material for your needs, showing you samples and explaining warranty differences.

3
Tear-Off & Deck Inspection

We remove the old roofing, inspect and repair the deck where needed, and ensure proper ventilation before installing the new system.

4
Installation & Cleanup

We install your new roof to manufacturer spec, perform a final quality check, and completely clean up the work site — including magnet sweeping for nails.
