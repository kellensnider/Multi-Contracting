Siding Eugene, OR

Durable Siding That Performs in the Pacific Northwest

Oregon's wet climate is tough on exterior siding. Years of rain, humidity, temperature swings, and moss growth take a toll on aging siding — leading to rot, warping, fading, and air infiltration that drives up your energy bills. When your siding starts to fail, it's not just a cosmetic problem: it's a structural and moisture risk.

Multi Contracting, LLC provides expert siding installation and replacement for homeowners across Eugene, Springfield, and Santa Clara, OR. We install durable siding products designed to handle the Pacific Northwest's demanding conditions while dramatically improving the appearance and value of your property. Whether you're replacing old wood siding, upgrading from aging vinyl, or siding a new addition, our team delivers clean, precise results.

As part of our full-service exterior contracting approach, siding projects can be coordinated alongside roofing or painting work — saving you time, money, and the hassle of managing multiple contractors. Call (541) 600-9570 for a free siding estimate today.

Our Siding Installation Process

1
Free Estimate & Consultation

We inspect your existing siding, assess any underlying damage, and discuss material options and colors that fit your style and budget.

2
Old Siding Removal

We carefully remove and dispose of existing siding, then inspect the sheathing and framing for any rot or moisture damage that should be addressed.

3
Moisture Barrier Installation

We install industry-standard house wrap to create a proper drainage plane — protecting your walls from water infiltration for decades.

4
Siding Installation & Trim

We install your new siding with precise measurements, proper flashing at windows and doors, and clean trim work that gives your home a finished, professional appearance.
