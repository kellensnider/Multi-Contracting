Roof Repair Eugene, OR

Stop Leaks Before They Get Worse

A small roof leak might seem manageable today, but left unaddressed it can lead to structural rot, mold growth, damaged insulation, and even ceiling collapse. At Multi Contracting, LLC, we take roof repair seriously — because your home's safety depends on a watertight roof.

Our experienced repair crews respond quickly to calls across Eugene, Springfield, and Santa Clara, OR. Whether you're dealing with a single missing shingle after a windstorm, cracked flashing around a chimney or skylight, pooling water on a flat roof, or widespread storm damage, we have the skills and materials to fix it right the first time.

We work on all roofing types — asphalt shingles, metal, tile, and flat membrane systems — and we carry full liability insurance and a current Oregon CCB license (CCB#238954). Not sure if your roof needs repair or full replacement? We'll give you an honest assessment with no pressure. Call (541) 600-9570 or request a free inspection today.
