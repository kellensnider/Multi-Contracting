Commercial Roofing Eugene, OR

Commercial Roofing Solutions for Lane County Businesses

Commercial roofing presents unique challenges that residential work simply doesn't require. Flat and low-slope roofs demand specialized waterproofing knowledge, proper drainage design, and materials engineered for large-scale performance. A commercial roof failure doesn't just leak — it can halt business operations, damage inventory, expose employees and customers to hazards, and result in significant liability.

Multi Contracting, LLC has the expertise and equipment to handle commercial roofing projects of all sizes in Eugene, Springfield, and Santa Clara, OR. We work with TPO (thermoplastic polyolefin), EPDM rubber membrane, modified bitumen, and commercial metal panel systems. Each material has distinct advantages depending on your building's footprint, slope, drainage, and budget — and our team will help you make the best decision for your property.

We schedule commercial projects to minimize disruption to your business, and we communicate clearly with property managers and building owners throughout every phase of the project. Call (541) 600-9570 for a free commercial roofing estimate in Lane County, OR.
