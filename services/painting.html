    Refresh your home or business inside and out. Multi Contracting, LLC delivers quality painting with proper prep work and premium products for results that last — serving Eugene, Springfield, and Santa Clara, OR.

    Painting Services Eugene, OR

    Clean, Lasting Paint Work — Inside and Out

    A fresh coat of paint is one of the quickest, most cost-effective ways to transform the look and feel of a home or business. But the difference between paint that looks great for years and paint that peels in one Oregon winter comes down to preparation. We take prep work seriously — because a great finish starts long before the brush ever touches the surface.

    Multi Contracting, LLC provides both interior and exterior painting services to homeowners and commercial property managers throughout Eugene, Springfield, and Santa Clara, OR. For exterior work, we clean and prep surfaces, repair any damaged wood or substrate, prime where needed, and apply weather-resistant coatings designed to withstand Oregon's wet climate. For interior work, we protect your furnishings and floors, patch and sand surfaces, and deliver smooth, consistent coverage in the colors and finishes you choose.

    Painting can also be coordinated with siding installation or other exterior work through our full-service contracting approach. Call (541) 600-9570 for a free estimate.

    Our Painting Process

    1
    Free Estimate & Color Consultation

    We assess your project, provide a written estimate, and discuss your color preferences and finish options.

    2
    Surface Preparation

    We clean, repair, sand, caulk gaps and cracks, and apply primer to ensure excellent adhesion and a smooth final surface.

    3
    Paint Application

    We apply paint in proper coats, allowing adequate drying time between each, and use the right tools (brush, roller, or sprayer) for each surface type.

    4
    Final Inspection & Cleanup

    We do a final walkthrough with you, touch up any areas that need it, and fully clean up — including removing protective coverings and disposing of materials.
