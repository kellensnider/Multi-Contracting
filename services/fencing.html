    Chain Link Fencing Eugene, OR

    Affordable, Long-Lasting Security Fencing for Eugene Properties

    Chain link fencing remains one of the most cost-effective, durable, and low-maintenance fencing options for both residential and commercial properties in the Eugene area. Whether you're securing a backyard for pets and children, enclosing a commercial site, protecting a sports field or school facility, or just defining your property boundaries, chain link delivers reliable security without the ongoing maintenance demands of wood or vinyl fencing.

    Multi Contracting, LLC has installed and repaired chain link fencing throughout Eugene, Springfield, and Santa Clara, OR. Our team works efficiently to complete installations on time and on budget — with proper post setting, tensioning, and gate installation for a fence that functions flawlessly for years to come.

    We also respond to emergency fence repairs. Whether a vehicle collision, storm damage, or vandalism has damaged your fence, we'll assess the situation and restore your security quickly. Call (541) 600-9570 anytime for a free fencing estimate or repair assessment.

    Our Fencing Process

    1
    Free Estimate & Layout

    We measure your property, identify underground utilities, and provide a written estimate covering all materials, labor, and gate hardware.

    2
    Post Setting

    We dig and concrete-set terminal and line posts at correct spacing and depth to ensure structural integrity throughout the fence line.

    3
    Fabric & Hardware Installation

    We attach and tension the chain link fabric, install all top rails, tension wire, ties, and hardware — including barbed wire or privacy slats if requested.

    4
    Gate Hanging & Final Check

    We hang and adjust all gates, verify the entire fence is plumb and properly tensioned, and clean up the work site before leaving.
